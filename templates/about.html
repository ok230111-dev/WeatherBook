About the WeatherBook

This WeatherBook allows users to check the weather conditions in any city worldwide. With real-time data from trusted weather sources, you can get up-to-date information on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather description.

Features

  • Check the current weather for any city in the world.
  • View detailed information such as temperature, humidity, and wind speed.
  • Get weather descriptions, such as "Clear sky" or "Cloudy".
  • Switch between metric and imperial units for temperature and wind speed.
  • Intuitive interface for easy access to weather information.

Technologies Used

WeatherBook is built using Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It fetches real-time weather data from the OpenWeatherMap API, ensuring accurate and up-to-date weather forecasts. The WeatherBook is designed to be responsive and accessible across devices, providing users with a seamless experience for monitoring weather conditions.
